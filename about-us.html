Our firm

A full-service CPA firm built on experience and trust

Tatiya CPA PC is comprised of highly qualified and experienced certified public accountants offering a complete range of accounting, tax, auditing, and business consulting services.

We serve a diverse clientele — from small local businesses to multi-state corporations, and individual professionals working in real estate, software design and consulting, and many other industries.

What sets us apart is the personal relationships we build with our clients. We take the time to understand your goals so we can provide proactive, tailored advice that goes beyond the numbers.

At a glance

  • Licensed CPAs with multidisciplinary experience
  • Personalized service for individuals and businesses
  • Open year round — not just at tax time
  • Available evenings and weekends
  • Trusted by clients across many industries
